Our Story

The Wedding Cake Shoppe in Jakarta is a family- owned and operated speciality cake shoppe and bakery. Owned and operated by sisters Cindy and Sarah. Founded by Cindy, who graduated The baking and pastry arts program at George Brown College. Furthering her love of sugar work at The Notter School of Pastry Arts, in Florida. Cindy had the wonderful opportunity of working side by side making wedding cakes with Bonnie Gordon. In 2010, Cindy and Sarah had the honour to be the first Canadians featured on Food Network Challenge, winning a silver medal. Le Couple Cake has been established since 2005, and the retail store has been open since 2009.
